About Me

My name is Maxwell (Max for short), and I'm a passionate video game programmer. I started developing video games in my late high school period in the form of mods in Minecraft. Since then, I began developing games in Unity for 2+ years during my studies at University and began switching to CRYENGINE in the summer of 2022.

Developing software for games has always brought enjoyment to myself and furthered my need for more challenges in the field. During most of my free time, I create my own games consisting of a variety of carefully detailed systems, tools, and mechanics. I especially take a great deal of effort to ensure that my programs are at a clean optimized level, while offering the highest quality of innovative and creative outputs.
